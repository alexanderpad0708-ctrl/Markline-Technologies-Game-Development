What is Markline Dimensions?

Markline Dimensions is a kid‑friendly gaming initiative focused on safe, fun, and creative play. It’s designed to be a bright, welcoming space where kids and families can enjoy games and activities without unsafe content or toxic behavior.

Every donation supports real‑world causes:

  • 60% to St. Jude’s cancer research
  • 20% to development of Markline Dimensions
  • 20% to scout camps (Girl Scouts & Boy Scouts)
